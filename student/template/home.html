About Our College



V V college is located on the easternmost part of Palakkad district, only 17 kms from Palakkad town and less than 10 kms from our border with Tamil Nadu, Walayar. It is easily reachable through NH544 connecting Palakkad with Coimbatore. The college campus sprawls over 17 acres of land providing enough space for any level of future growth and development. Situated away from urban congestion and pollution, the campus offers an ideal serene atmosphere for learning. It is surrounded by a picturesque landscape comprising paddy fields, green bushes and grassland, and ranges of the Walayar hills in the background with forest clad valleys and trickling streams. Planted greenery around the college adds up to the beauty. Plentiful supply of pure water drawn out of a perennial open well is an added blessing, particularly in a place like Palakkad. V V College, Kanjikode which has made a humble start with just three UG programmes in 2002, now proudly functions with 10 UG and 2 PG programmes. The programmes of study have been selected with a proper awareness regarding their relevance and importance in the current world scenario. Programmes in Science, commerce, business, and Arts are offered. The teaching and learning process has been so designed that it meets the vision and mission envisaged by the management.
